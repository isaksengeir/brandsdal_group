Software

Application of computers to solve complex chemical and biological questions are the heart of our activities. In many cases, this requires highly advanced software as well as access to high performance computers.

Qgui

Q is the most used program in the Brandsdal group. Q was developed by the Ã…qvist group at Uppsala University.

We have developed a graphical user interface to Q - the Qgui. The interface simplifies the process of assigning missing force field parameters and building molecular topologies, create input files for molecular dynamics simulation and free energy calculations, as well as tools to analyze the generated data.

Qgui can be obtained by contacting the Brandsdal group, and is freely available.
