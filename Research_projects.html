Research projects

Enzymes from the cold

Life has to effectively colonized most of our planet, and extremophilic organisms require specalized enzymes to survice under harsh conditions. We focus our attention to so-called cold-loving organisms (psychrophilic) that express heat-labile enzymes with high specific activity and catalytic activity at low to moderate temperatures.

Antimicrobial peptides

Antimicrobial resistance is one of the major global threats to scociety today. Antibiotics have revolutionized the way patients with bacterial infections are treated and have contributed to the reduced mortality and morbidity from bacterial infections. Unfortunately, misuse of antibotics have led to the emergence and selection of resistant bacteria.
